🥚 Breakfast Configurations
🌯 Meal 1: The One-Pan Sunrise Scramble Burritos
• Ingredients: 1 half-dozen egg carton, 1 package of pre-cooked shelf-stable bacon slices, 1 bag
of shredded cheddar cheese, 1 pack of flour tortillas, travel salt/pepper.
• Required Equipment: 1 standard frying pan, 1 mixing spatula.
• Step-by-Step Execution: Dice the pre-cooked meat. Crack 4 to 5 eggs directly into the dry
frying pan, add a single teaspoon of drinking water, and stir in the meat pieces. Position the pan
over a medium flame, stirring continuously until cooked. Cut the flame, sprinkle cheese over the
top, cover to melt, and roll into flour tortillas.
