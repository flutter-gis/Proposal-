🥪 Light & Fast Lunch Playbook
🌮 Meal 1: The Classic Crispy Quesadillas
• Steps: Position one tortilla flat in your dry pan. Layer shredded cheese across one half. Drop
on drained canned chicken chunks and a spoonful of salsa. Fold the empty side over. Light your
stove to medium-low. Toast for 2-3 minutes per side until crunchy and golden brown.
🥑 Meal 2: Stealth Turkey & Avocado Wraps
• Steps: Completely no-cook! Lay a large tortilla flat. Layer 3-4 slices of deli turkey and
provolone cheese down the center line. Slice your fresh avocado and arrange slices over the
meat. Drizzle with honey mustard, fold the ends inward, roll into a tight log, and slice diagonally.
🍗 Meal 3: Loaded Chicken Salad Wraps
• Steps: Drain a large can of chicken into a bowl. Flake apart with a fork and mix in Mayo
packets, a handful of crunchy chopped slaw mix, and crumpled pre-cooked bacon bits. Shake in
salt and pepper. Roll the chilled chicken salad tightly into a flour tortilla.
🍕 Meal 4: Pesto Flatbread Thin-Crust Pizzas
• Steps: Take a raw tortilla and spread two heavy tablespoons of basil pesto smoothly across its
surface. Layer on thin disks of a fresh mozzarella log, tomato slices, and pepperoni. Drop into a
dry skillet over low heat, tent loosely with a sheet of foil to trap heat, and bake for 4 minutes until
the cheese bubbles.
🥖 Meal 5: The Dry-Box Italian Grinders
• Steps: Slice your crusty sub rolls lengthwise. Layer folds of salami, pepperoni, deli turkey, and
provolone cheese. Top with a handful of fresh slaw/lettuce mix and tomato slices. Drizzle Italian
dressing from home directly over the top right before eating.
🥗 Meal 6: Fiesta Black Bean & Corn Salad Bowls
• Steps: Combine half a can of rinsed black beans, half a can of sweet corn, a handful of finely
diced bell peppers, and chopped yellow onions in a large traveler bowl. Toss with a splash of
lime juice, salt, and table salsa. Scoop it up and eat it using salty tortilla chips!
🥓 Meal 7: Summer Orchard BLT Avocado Bowls
• Steps: Warm your pre-cooked bacon slices in your skillet for 45 seconds until snappy, then
crumble. Slice two fresh avocados in half and remove the central seeds. Toss your crumbled
bacon, chopped cherry tomatoes, and lettuce with a splash of dressing in a cup, then spoon it
directly into the hollow avocado centers! Eat with a spoon.
